About Us

Unveiled on 18th December 2017, Merawan Catering has been the leading purveyor of gourmet coffees in Jakarta, marked by our extensive range of exclusive top-quality food, cooked to perfection using a variety of cooking methods. Since then, our food menu has expanded to include gourmet delights - prepared and artfully arranged to satisfy the senses.

Merawan Catering experience is about blending art with lifestyle to give our guests the space to indulge in their own time and appreciate the finer things in life. All tcc art boutique caffès have an artistic, stylish, urbane and cosy atmosphere. With ingenious combination of lighting and colours, our art boutique caffès are also recognised for their bespoke furniture and exhibits of art pieces.

At tcc, take a step back from the buzzing city life by reclining on our posh furniture and be enveloped in pure sophistication and satisfaction. Enjoy d’Life.



Our Vision

To be the leading branded hospitality chain in Jakarta and the region through carefully monitored service quality and product standard.



Our Mission

Striving towards understanding and tailoring our products to suit our guests, We are constantly innovates and dedicate itself to satisfying the ever-changing tastes of patrons by producing delectable food and creative beverages to render a memorable and enjoyable experience.
